# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: September 22, 2025_

[REDACTED] respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the app.

## Information We Collect
- **User Content**: You can create and save content with optional context.  
- **No Tracking by Default**: Analytics and crash reporting are disabled by default.  
- **Optional Services**: You may choose to enable analytics or crash reporting in the app settings.

## How We Use Information
- To allow you to create, view, and share content with your private circle.  
- To improve app stability (if you chose to enable crash reporting).  
- To improve app features and user experience (if you chose to enable analytics).

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell your data.  
- We only share data with trusted service providers necessary for the operation of the app.

## Your Rights (GDPR)
- **Access and Portability**: You may request a copy of your data.  
- **Rectification**: You may update or correct your information.  
- **Deletion**: You may request the deletion of your data at any time.  
- **Withdrawal of Consent**: You may disable analytics or crash reporting at any time in the app settings.

## Contact
For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us here on GitHub
